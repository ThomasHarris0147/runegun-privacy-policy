Privacy Policy for RUNEGUN

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game RUNEGUN ("the App", "we", "us") handles information. RUNEGUN is a single-player, offline arena game. Our approach is simple: we do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information We Collect

None. RUNEGUN does not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. We have no servers and the App makes no network connections.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves your game progress — such as your souls balance, unlocked characters and skins, and settings like volume — locally on your device using your browser/WebView's local storage. This information:

  • never leaves your device;
  • is not transmitted to us or any third party;
  • is not personal or identifying;
  • can be cleared at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

No Ads, Analytics, or Tracking

RUNEGUN contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not use an advertising identifier and do not track you across apps or websites.

Permissions

The App does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or files. It reads your device's general browser/user-agent information only on-device to enable touch controls; this is not stored or sent anywhere.

Children's Privacy

RUNEGUN does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a revised "Last updated" date.
